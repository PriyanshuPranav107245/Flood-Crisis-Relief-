Frequently Asked Questions

Where does my donation go?

100% of your donation goes directly to emergency relief efforts including food, clean water, shelter, and medical supplies for affected families. We maintain complete transparency about fund allocation and provide detailed reports to all donors.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes! We are a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. You will receive a tax receipt for your donation via email within 24 hours, which you can use for tax deduction purposes.

Can I donate anonymously?

Yes, you can choose to make an anonymous donation during the checkout process. Just leave the donor information fields blank and provide only an email for tax receipts.

What if I want to donate supplies instead of money?

We welcome in-kind donations! Contact us at donate@floodrelief.org or call 1-800-RELIEF-1 for information about donating supplies like blankets, food, water, and medical kits. We'll coordinate collection and delivery.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover). You can also donate via bank transfer, PayPal, and cryptocurrency. Email donate@floodrelief.org for alternative payment methods.

How long will the relief efforts continue?

We operate in phases: emergency response (0-3 months), stabilization (3-12 months), and reconstruction (1-3 years). Long-term donations help us support communities through all stages of recovery.

How can I cancel my monthly donation?

You can cancel your monthly donation at any time by contacting us at donate@floodrelief.org or calling 1-800-RELIEF-1. You can also manage your subscription through your account dashboard on our website.

Can I receive updates on how my donation was used?

Absolutely! Check the "Send me updates about the relief efforts" option during donation to receive monthly impact reports showing exactly how your donation helped affected families.

Does your organization have any certifications or credentials?

Yes, we are registered with the IRS as a 501(c)(3) nonprofit and hold certifications from Charity Navigator, GiveWell, and the Better Business Bureau's Wise Giving Alliance. You can verify our status on Guidestar.org.

Can my employer match my donation?

Many employers offer donation matching programs. We work with most major companies. Check with your HR department and mention our nonprofit ID (EIN: 12-3456789) to set up matching.

How can I get involved as a volunteer?

We need volunteers for disaster relief, reconstruction, and administrative support. Please visit our website or email volunteer@floodrelief.org to learn about current opportunities in your area.

Can I donate in a different currency?

Our online system processes USD, but we accept CAN, EUR, GBP, and other major currencies. Your bank will handle the conversion. Contact donate@floodrelief.org for wire transfer options in other currencies.

Is there a minimum or maximum donation amount?

There is no minimum donation amount—every dollar helps! For donations over $10,000, please contact our major donors team at majordonors@floodrelief.org for specialized support and recognition options.

When will aid reach affected communities?

We distribute emergency aid within 48-72 hours of receiving donations to critical needs areas. Local partnerships ensure fast, efficient delivery to families who need help most urgently.

How is your organization funded?

We operate entirely on donations from individuals, corporations, and foundations. We maintain an operating reserve of 3 months of expenses to ensure continuity of aid during crises.
